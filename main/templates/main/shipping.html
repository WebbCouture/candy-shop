Shipping & Returns

📦 Shipping

We process and ship orders within 1–2 business days after payment is confirmed.

Delivery times vary by location:

  • Domestic: 2–5 business days
  • International: 7–21 business days

Shipping fees are calculated at checkout and depend on your order weight and destination.

↩️ Returns

If you are not fully satisfied with your purchase, you may return unopened and unused items within 14 days of delivery for a full refund.

To start a return, please contact our customer service team with your order number and reason for return.

Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after receiving your returned item.

Note: Perishable or custom-made products are not eligible for return.
