🍭 The Art of Hand-Poured Lollipops

Published: Aug 1, 2025 • 4 min read

Our rainbow lollipops start with premium cane sugar and real fruit flavors. Each batch is poured by hand, which means no two lollies are exactly alike. That uniqueness is our signature — a tiny edible work of art.

  • Natural flavors where possible
  • Small-batch pouring for consistency
  • Slow-cool for shine and snap

🍫 Choosing the Right Chocolate

Published: Jul 20, 2025 • 3 min read

Cocoa percentage isn’t everything. For truffles, we prioritize smooth melt and balanced bitterness. For bark and bars, we layer crunchy add-ins for contrast.

Tip: Store chocolate in a cool, dry place (15–18°C). Avoid the fridge unless it’s a heatwave.
